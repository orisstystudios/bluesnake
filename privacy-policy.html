🐍 BlueSnake — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

ℹ️ Host this file at a public URL (e.g. GitHub Pages) and enter that URL in the Google Play Console and the app's Info screen.

This Privacy Policy explains how the BlueSnake mobile game ("the App"), provided by Orissty Studios ("we", "us"), handles information. We designed BlueSnake to respect your privacy: we do not collect, store, or share personal information on our own servers, and the App has no user accounts or logins.

1. Information stored on your device

BlueSnake saves a small amount of game data locally on your device only (using your browser/WebView local storage). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us. It includes:

  • Your best score and earned coins
  • Which characters you own and have selected
  • Your settings (difficulty, sound on/off)

You can erase this data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the App.

2. Advertising

BlueSnake may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information, such as your device's advertising identifier, approximate location (derived from IP), and app interactions, in order to show and measure ads. We do not control and do not receive this data directly.

  • Learn how Google uses information from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can reset or limit ad personalisation in your device settings under Google → Ads.

3. Purchases

If you buy in-app items (such as removing ads, characters, or coin packs), payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment card details. Purchases are governed by Google Play's terms and privacy policy.

4. Children's privacy

BlueSnake is a family-friendly arcade game suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If ads are shown to users in regions with children's privacy rules, ad content is configured accordingly.

5. Data security

Because game data stays on your device and we operate no servers or accounts, there is no personal data held by us that could be exposed.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at orisstystudios@gmail.com.
